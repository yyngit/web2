Welcome to the information section of our mission. We strive to clean up our shorelines through three simple and effective steps.

Project Description
Our first step is to start combating the garbage in our oceans is to start at the source...our beaches.
Our next step is to fundraise enough money to fund more beach side cleanups and to support more non-profit corporations like us.
Our final step is to spread our message to everyone to bring awareness to the growing issue.
